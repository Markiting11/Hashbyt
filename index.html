Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline? +

Most Minimum Viable Product (MVP) projects, including discovery, design, and development, range from 10 to 16 weeks. Comprehensive enterprise solutions require a more detailed timeline based on complexity.

How are your web app development costs structured? +

We primarily work on a fixed-bid basis for clearly defined projects or a time-and-materials model for long-term engagements or projects with evolving scopes. Our free audit helps define the best model for you.

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance? +

Absolutely. We offer a 90-day bug warranty and provide optional monthly retainer packages that cover monitoring, feature updates, security patches, and scaling support.
